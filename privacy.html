    Privacy Policy

  • Information We Collect: We collect limited information from children to provide a safe and engaging experience. The types of information we may collect include:

    • Basic User Information: Children may provide a username or alias to personalize their experience on our platform.
    • Usage Data: We collect information about how children interact with our website, such as the activities they engage in and the features they use.

  • How We Use Children's Information: The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of enhancing the educational experience for children. We use the information to:

    • Personalize Content: We use usernames or aliases to personalize the website experience.
    • Improve Services: Usage data helps us understand how children use our platform, enabling us to enhance and improve our content and features.

  • Data Sharing and Security: We do not share children's personal information with third parties. We take appropriate measures to protect children's data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

  • Parental Consent and Control: We require parental consent for children to use our services. Parents can review, update, or delete their child's information by contacting us at info@bachpanplus.com. Parents can also request the deletion of their child's account.

  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies for technical purposes, such as ensuring a smooth user experience. We do not use cookies to collect personal information from children.

  • Links to External Websites: Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage parents to review the privacy policies of any linked sites.

  • Changes to Privacy Policy: We may update our Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify parents about significant changes and obtain any necessary parental consent.

  • Contact Us: If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or your child's information, please contact us at info@bachpanplus.com.
