Teaching Methodology

Education is highly valued and considered a lifelong pursuit. Islamic teachings emphasize the importance of seeking knowledge, both religious and worldly, as a means of drawing closer to Allah (God), understanding His creation, and contributing positively to society.

Quran

The Quran is a source of spiritual guidance, ethical principles, and legal foundations, inspiring Muslims to cultivate a deeper understanding of their faith and live in accordance with its teachings.

Hadith

The teachings of Hadith, offer a comprehensive ethical framework emphasizing compassion, justice, humility, and kindness, providing a model for Muslims to emulate in their daily lives.

Character

Character building involves cultivating traits such as sincerity, patience, honesty, and humility, with the intention of attaining spiritual growth and embodying the teachings of the faith in one's actions and interactions.

Dawah

Dawah refers to the act of conveying the message of Islam to others through communication, behavior, and exemplifying the faith's principles in order to invite understanding and acceptance.
